Membership

We offer five different levels of membership spanning the two ranges - Shooters Indoor Range & Gun Shop and S.H.A.R.P.E. Shooters Outdoor Range.

5 Levels

These five levels are Indoor Range, Outdoor Range, Combination of Indoor Range & Outdoor Range, Club (combination plus more perks), and Founder (club plus more perks).

Founder Limited Availability

The Founder level membership will only be offered through 2015 where those who start at the Founder level will maintain their Founder member status as long as annual renewals are purchased.

Options

Each membership level has an Individual, Family, and Life option.

Application

Download our Membership Application to view the definitions of each membership level.

Become a Member Today

Simply bring the filled out Membership Application to either Shooters Indoor Range & Gun Shop or S.H.A.R.P.E. Shooters to join our membership ranks!

One Time Waiver

One of the perks of being a member is that the Range Rules and Liability Waiver is signed one time as part of the membership application process and kept on file. This allows for the by-passing of the daily sign-in when you come to shoot.

Members Only Lounge

At S.H.A.R.P.E. Shooters we have a designated "Members Only Lounge" where members can sit back and relax to watch the ball games on the flat screens, play some cards, or simply enjoy the comaraderie of fellow members.

Lockers

We also offer lockers to rent to store your range bags and other range items so that you won't have to lug them between home and the range. Lockers are available at $5 per month on a first-come first-serve basis.
