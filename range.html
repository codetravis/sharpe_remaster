Pistol Bays

Our facility currently has 4 pistol bays - two of which are 30 yards deep and two that are 50 yards deep. Don't forget the archers out there - we can easily and quickly configure a pistol bay for archery as well.

Archery

We can easily and quickly configure a pistol bay for archery. We have 3-D targets (blocks and deer). Charges for our 3-D targets are: $3 Small Block, $5 Large Block, $8 Deer.

Rifle Range

We have constructed a 100-yard and a 200-yard rifle range that will support shooting from bench and prone positions. We are working with the NRA and other shooting organizations to attract and host both sanctioned and club/social shoots such as 3-gun, IDPA, Steel Challenge, and more.

Range Fees and Rentals

$15 for the first person on a bench, $10 for each additional person on the same bench. Kids 15 and under are $5. If you want to try out a gun, we have serveral available for rent

Eyes and Ears

Ear and Eye protection are required and you can use your own, or we have them for sale and rent ($1.50 each to rent).

Targets

Targets vary in cost from $0.75 up to $1.50. You may bring your own targets if you like.

Safety Video

All patrons shooting on our range are required to watch our safety video as it is a liability insurance requirement of ours.

Challenge Card

This is a one-time watch where a challenge card will be given for watching the video. Please bring this card with you on subsequent visits to show our staff as we cannot remember everyone.

Lost Card

If you forget or lose your challenge card, we'll entertain you with our safety video again and give you another challenge card.

Daily Specials

5 days a week we have a daily special where the first person lane fee is $10

  • Monday - Senior Citizens (60yrs +)
  • Tuesday - Ladies
  • Wednesday - First Responders (fire fighters, EMT, law enforcement)
  • Thursday - Military (active duty, retirees, and veterans)
  • Friday - College Student
